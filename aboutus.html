Andrew Izumi and Arthur

Our Recent History


August

It is finally spring-time here in Hobart. I have been working on the house and garden. Mainly catching up on all the things I couldn't do for the past 12 months while working overseas. In fact, I have a little too much time on my hands. I would prefer to be sail designing but it seems there is not so much demand for high-performance sails in Tasmania.

June

Finally the Volvo Ocean Race is finished. At least for Telefonica where the boats are locked into 3rd and 6th place. Quite disappointing for a 2-boat program suffering from ineffective management. Good people supporting the sailors but poorly utilised. But that takes nothing away from the good job the Ericsson team did to finish 1st and 4th.
Also Puma team managed to hang in behind Tele blue until their wheels fell off and Puma took second place.

At home, I now have some time to finish some jobs around the house. One is the work bench in the workshop. This should be finished in a week. That will free up space to work efficiently on other building projects.
